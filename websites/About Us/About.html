About Us

�Friendship is the hardest thing in the world to explain. It�s not something that you learn in school. But if you haven�t learnt the meaning of friendship, you haven�t learned anything.�

We are a bunch of normal but diverse people that chose to stick together since we met during our school days. We all are unique in our own ways and excel in our fields of preference. Together we share a journey from childhood to our teenage years, from the terrifying final moments of an important exam to the intensifying moments of the final free kick in a football game. Over the years, we have had thrilling trips, chilling sleep overs, surprise birthday parties, movie and game sessions and our fair share of fights and beefs. And as the days will go onward, we might even be in different corners of the world. But throughout the ups and downs, victories and defeats, pranks and endeavors, we chose to nurture our friendship. And it has consistently been growing for the last fifteen years and still counting.
